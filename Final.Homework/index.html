HOW TO CHOOSE

Match your case with your lifestyle

If you are kind of person who enjoys outside walks, camping, climbing and etc. what you need is durable case. So if bear charges you can stuck your iphone between bear's jaws and be sure our Extra Durable cases will do it's job. Explore new places with new case.

Make your priorities

We already discuss durable cases. If you spent a lot time in a car or just love simple things, go with cases that are compatible with wireless charging. Same goes with Magsafe. So think a bit and decide what you need the most.

Jack of all trades

By spending little bit more, you can choose case that comes with all the benefits. Durability, stylish design and compatibility. Unlike us humans, our cases really can be good at everything, but in this case we have to charge you more. So you have to think about your life choices.
